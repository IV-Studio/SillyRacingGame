# Extra Ideas

## Near-Term Iteration Targets

- Test a harsher fuel failure rule where unaffordable dice force a stall or backfire.
- Revisit shortcuts later if the base single-route race starts to feel too deterministic.
- Try a fourth dice row that mixes 3 yellow/orange dice and rewards consistency.
- Add abilities that convert coins into fuel or movement during late-game turns.
- Add mystery cards that interact with opponents instead of only self-buffs.

## Metrics To Watch

- Whether the red die creates exciting comebacks or just feels punitive.
- Whether players hoard coins too often for the ability row instead of racing.
- Whether trap density meaningfully changes route tempo.
- Whether first player becomes too important in 2-player games.
